Thought Dec 2025
On Learning to Code Without a Roadmap
Three months in. No bootcamp, no CS degree, no mentor physically nearby. Just docs, YouTube at 1.5x speed, and a lot of broken scripts. The thing nobody tells you: confusion is the learning. When you're confused, your brain is building the map. The clarity comes later, all at once, when you least expect it. Keep going.
