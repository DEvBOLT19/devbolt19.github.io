// HOW TO PLAY

PROJECT RuTHER — GUIDE v1.0

01
Flappy Bird
Press SPACE or tap the canvas to flap. Avoid pipes. Tap again after game over to retry.
02
Wordle NYT-style
Type a 5-letter word and press ENTER. 🟩 correct spot · 🟨 wrong spot · ⬛ not in word. You have 6 tries.
03
Endless Runner
Press SPACE or tap to jump. Double-jump supported. Collect gold coins for bonus points.
04
Platform Jumper
Use ← → or A D to move. Auto-jump when landing on platforms. Reach the highest height.
05
Clicker Game
Click the ⚡ to earn points. Spend them on upgrades to generate points per second automatically.
06
Mini Shooter
Click targets before the ring shrinks to zero. Smaller targets = more points. 30 seconds on the clock.
07
Match 3
Click a gem, then click an adjacent gem to swap them. Match 3+ in a row or column to score. Chain reactions multiply points.
08
Sudoku
Click a cell, then press a number key or use the numpad. Fill every row, column, and 3×3 box with 1–9 without repeating.
09
Pixel Painter
Select a color from the palette, then draw on the 24×24 canvas. Use Fill (🪣) to flood-fill areas. Save as PNG.
10
Physics Sandbox
Select a shape and click the canvas to spawn physics bodies. Hit 💥 Explode to launch everything outward.
??
Hidden (Easter Egg)
Open the Hidden game. Type the Konami Code on your keyboard: ↑ ↑ ↓ ↓ ← → ← → B A to unlock Snake. Use arrow keys or WASD to play.
