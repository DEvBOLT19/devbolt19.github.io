[ About Me ]

Creative developer based in nowhere & everywhere.

I'm Aiden — a designer who codes, or a developer who designs, depending on the day. For the last six years I've been helping studios, startups, and independent brands build digital experiences that feel considered rather than loud.

My work lives in the dark end of the spectrum: monochrome palettes, generous whitespace, film grain, and typography that does the heavy lifting. I believe restraint is a creative act.

When I'm not shipping, I'm making generative art, collecting vinyl, and chasing the perfect cup of black coffee.
